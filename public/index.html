Thoughts

On AI in Development

AI is a tool — a powerful one, but a tool nonetheless. I enjoy problem-solving: not just arriving at a solution, but understanding how it is built and why it works the way it does. There is something lost when AI handles the details, and I think that matters. That said, the industry is moving and I am moving with it. The developers who thrive will be those who use AI with judgement, not those who outsource their thinking to it.
